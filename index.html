Mercedes-Benz C-Class Details

Description

The Mercedes-Benz C-Class is a line of luxury compact executive cars produced by Mercedes-Benz. Introduced in 1993 as a replacement for the 190 (W201) range, the C-Class has been available with a "traditional" sedan/saloon, a station wagon/estate, a coupé, and a convertible version.

This particular 2023 C-Class sedan represents the latest generation, known for its elegant design, luxurious interior, and refined driving experience. With a powerful diesel engine, it delivers both performance and efficiency for discerning drivers.
